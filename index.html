Meditation Healing

"Meditation Healing" is a project for busy people to have a mindfulness and healing experience. Nowadays, modern people live under high pressure, work in fast paced environments, and focus heavily on their job and careers. We have a lot on our plate in a day and sometimes multitask in a short period of time. Due to these kinds of stressful and fast movements, our body is more prone to fatigue and sickness. The busy state of being influences our body, soul, and spirit negatively. So we are a group of students from Parsons that interest in how to combine technology (what we learn) and meditation methodology together, and brings a new way of relaxation and experience body healing.
