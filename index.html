Meditation Healing

"Meditaion Healing" is service for busy people to have a relax, and mindful experience. Players use their smartphone as the controller to interact with three rotating objects on a sharing screen. Through synchronizing and manipulating the speed and shape of the objects, players can focus on the moment together, and have a relaxed and refreshing moment.

It is an experience for three people at a time, so please find another two companies to experience with you. Wait for a moment until your turn. If you want to see the screen, choose the viewer. If you want to interact, choose the user.
